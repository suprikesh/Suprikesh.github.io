About Me

Where Science Meets the Soil

I am a PhD Research Scholar at Murdoch University, Perth, working at the intersection of Biotechnology and Bioinformatics applied to agricultural challenges. My research journey has spanned ICAR institutions, permaculture design, and field agronomy across India before arriving in Australia.

With 13+ peer-reviewed publications, a Government of India copyright patent, and the prestigious GRDC Research Scholarship, I bring both theoretical rigour and hands-on field expertise to every project.

I believe in bridging the gap between cutting-edge science and practical farming — making sustainable agriculture not just possible, but profitable and scalable.

Sustainable agriculture is not a compromise between food security and environmental stewardship — it is the only path where both can thrive. My mission is to bridge the gap between traditional farming wisdom and cutting-edge biotechnology.
